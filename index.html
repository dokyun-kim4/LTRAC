Project Description

Our objective was to make an easy way of keeping track of workouts as well as to gamify working out.
We hope to motivate more people to maintain a healthy and consistent lifestyle.

Feature Descriptions

  1. Customizable profile
  2. View & edit all your routines in one page
  3. Work out and level up
  4. Easy access to all workout history
